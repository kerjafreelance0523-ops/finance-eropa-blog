Marcus Reinhardt is a senior markets strategist with over fifteen years of experience across European equities, fixed income, and digital assets. He has held senior trading and research roles at institutional desks in Frankfurt and London, and now leads strategy and content at EuroCoinInvest Research.

His work focuses on Eurozone macro, cross-asset signals, and risk-aware positioning for private and professional investors. He has contributed to institutional research on ECB policy, European energy markets, and the integration of crypto and traditional portfolios. Outside markets, he writes on financial literacy and long-term wealth building for European audiences.
