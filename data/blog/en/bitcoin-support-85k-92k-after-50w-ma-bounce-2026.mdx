IG’s market navigator adds that the recent sharp rebound could be a dead cat bounce until Bitcoin reclaims \$81K. That ties the \$85K–92K support to the \$81K reclaim: holding support in the \$85K–92K band on a pullback would set up a potential push back above \$81K, while losing the band would make a reclaim of \$81K much harder.
