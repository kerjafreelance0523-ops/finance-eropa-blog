After a break below the 50-week MA and a bounce, price often needs time to resolve. So the Bitcoin 50 week MA bounce is context, not a guarantee of a straight move higher. The Bitcoin choppy range 80K phase is where we find out whether the bounce will hold or fail. IG’s market navigator adds that until Bitcoin reclaims \$81K the bias can remain bearish; that implies the choppy range could persist until we get a clear breakout above \$81K or a break below support.
